ABOUT US

The Regalia Group Corporation a subsidiary of ASEC Group of companies. The Regalia Park Tower C is a 35 Floor Mixed Use Condominium Tower conveniently located at the Heart of Quezon City just a stone throw away from Cubao Araneta Center, Gateway Mall, LRT Line 2 and MRT Cubao Stations, EDSA Highway, cluster of malls, commercial and recreation centers, schools and churches. The Tower comes with the following features designed specifically to satisfy your taste.

Complete with amenities like landscaped driveway & rotunda, shops and snack stores, centralized reception lobby, 4 hi-speed elevators, laundromat & dryer facilities, 24 Hour CCTVs, intercom units and security, cable & telephone ready, pocket gardens & landscaped spaces, scenic hallway den with xeriscape garden , centralized garbage system, back up power supply, 2 floors of amenity facilities, infinity pool, prayer room, kiddie playroom, gym,function rooms, and sky harden with jogging path at the top of the tower. Enjoy the majestic 360 degree view of the metropolis (while relaxing in the infinity pool, jogging or just chilling out in the sky garden, passing by the scenic hallways on glass window corridors or just in the comfort of your own unit).
